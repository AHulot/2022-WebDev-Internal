Passwords and Security

As cyber security threats become more frequent, it’s useful to know what you can do to secure your personal information online. Although there is no way to fully prevent cyber security attacks, there are ways to reduce the risk. Here are a few basic tips…

Because of increasing cybercrime, it is important to make sure all devices are secure. These include computers, cell/mobile phones, and others which are a part of our daily lives. Emails, phone numbers, text messages, and other kinds of sensitive information are all stored in our devices, which makes securing them very important.
